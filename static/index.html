  • Grant Gustavson

    Grant Gustavson is the co-founder of MB Computing. In addition to his ventures in cryptocurrency, he is the principal of Authentic Residential, LLC a single family rental platform with 300 homes across 5 markets in the western USA. Grant currently serves on the board of Public Storage Canada. Grant is Principal of American Commercial Equities (ACE), which owns and operates commercial rental space in Southern California, and is Chairman of Authentic Capital Group, a real estate investment firm. Grant began his career working in acquisitions for American Homes 4 Rent (NYSE: AMH), a publicly traded single-family REIT. Grant earned his bachelor’s degree in Business Administration from the University of Southern California’s Marshall School of Business.

    Brandon Flannery

    Brandon Flannery is a founding partner of MB Computing. Mr. Flannery brings a deep expertise of quantitative research, machine learning, and crypto development to the MB Computing team. Prior to founding MB Computing, Brandon helped build out the investment infrastructure and research capabilities of Pluribus Labs, a systematic active equity manager. Pluribus Labs was acquired by Golden Gate Capital in 2017 and again by Exos Financial in 2022. Brandon has spearheaded the development of MB Computing's investment research capabilities, allowing MBC to underwrite investment opportunities across digital assets, energy, and carbon ecosystems. He holds a bachelor's degree in statistics from University of California, Berkeley.

    Ryan Lowery

    Ryan Lowery joined MB Computing as the group’s Acquisition Officer in 2021. Mr. Lowery is responsible for power and site procurement along with assisting in various other aspects of the business, including establishing relationships with power producers and assisting in site negotiations, as well as establishing relationships with external mining entities to procure hosting contracts and asset acquisitions. Prior to joining MB Computing, Mr. Lowery was a power trader and natural gas analyst for several prominent commodity trading firms after graduating from the University of Houston with a Bachelor of Science degree in Industrial Engineering. Mr. Lowery is based in Houston, Texas.
