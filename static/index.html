MB Computing

A Bitcoin Mining Company

MB Computing is a computing services provider focused on supporting the growth of decentralized networks and applications globally.

MB Computing recognizes the strengthening global demand for computing resources. We develop and operate data centers across the globe that address growing needs for affordable hosting services, sustainable energy resources, geographically diversified computing facilities, and trustworthy, experienced management.

MB Computing’s core business is self mining. The teams’ industry expertise also allows it to provide ancillary services to its customers that include colocation, equipment sourcing, site development, and ESG advisory.

Services

Bitcoin Mining

Utilizing the most cost-efficient, sustainable energy sources in North America, MBC consistently delivers carbon-optimized Bitcoin income with industry leading margins.

Colocation

MBC leverages its network of farm operators and current deployments to provide clients sustainable, affordable access to best-in-class computing facilities.

Equipment Sourcing

We maintain direct relationships with manufacturers and logistics providers to deliver clients equipment quickly and at wholesale prices.

Site Development

We design and constructs mining facilities uniquely equipped with competitive power pricing and positive environmental positioning.

Carbon Advisory

MBC focusses on producing carbon-optimized Bitcoin. We are active participants in carbon markets globally. We help clients build operations that maximize returns while minimizing environmental impact.

Financing

MBC has supported several industry veterans in strengthening Bitcoin's infrastructure. We are constantly looking to support innovators with flexible financing solutions.

Team

Grant Gustavson


Grant Gustavson is the co-founder of MB Computing. In addition to his ventures in cryptocurrency, he is the principal of Authentic Residential, LLC a single family rental platform with 300 homes across 5 markets in the western USA. Grant currently serves on the board of Public Storage Canada. Grant is Principal of American Commercial Equities (ACE), which owns and operates commercial rental space in Southern California, and is Chairman of Authentic Capital Group, a real estate investment firm. Grant began his career working in acquisitions for American Homes 4 Rent (NYSE: AMH), a publicly traded single-family REIT. Grant earned his bachelor’s degree in Business Administration from the University of Southern California’s Marshall School of Business.

Brandon Flannery


Brandon Flannery is a founding partner of MB Computing. Mr. Flannery brings a deep expertise of quantitative research, machine learning, and crypto development to the MB Computing team. Prior to founding MB Computing, Brandon helped build out the investment infrastructure and research capabilities of Pluribus Labs, a systematic active equity manager. Pluribus Labs was acquired by Golden Gate Capital in 2017 and again by Exos Financial in 2022. Brandon has spearheaded the devleopment of MB Computing's trading capabilities, allowing MBC to deploy alternative strategies across crypto and carbon ecosystems. He holds a bachelor's degree in statistics from University of California, Berkeley.

Ryan Lowery


Ryan Lowery joined MB Computing as the group’s Acquisition Officer in 2021. Mr. Lowery is responsible for power and site procurement along with assisting in various other aspects of the business, including establishing relationships with power producers and assisting in site negotiations, as well as establishing relationships with external mining entities to procure hosting contracts and asset acquisitions. Prior to joining MB Computing, Mr. Lowery was a power trader and natural gas analyst for several prominent commodity trading firms after graduating from the University of Houston with a Bachelor of Science degree in Industrial Engineering. Mr. Lowery is based in Houston, Texas.
