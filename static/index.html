MB Computing

A Bitcoin Mining Company

MB Computing is a computing services provider focused on supporting the growth of decentralized networks and applications globally.

MB Computing recognizes the strengthening global demand for computing resources. We develop and operate data centers across the globe that address growing needs for affordable hosting services, sustainable energy resources, geographically diversified computing facilities, and trustworthy, experienced management.

MB Computing’s core business is self mining. The teams’ industry expertise also allows it to provide ancillary services to its customers that include colocation, equipment sourcing, site development, and ESG advisory.

Services

Bitcoin Mining

Utilizing the most cost-efficient, sustainable energy sources in North America, MBC consistently delivers carbon-optimized Bitcoin income with industry leading margins.

Colocation

MBC leverages its network of farm operators and current deployments to provide clients sustainable, affordable access to best-in-class computing facilities.

Equipment Sourcing

We maintain direct relationships with manufacturers and logistics providers to deliver clients equipment quickly and at wholesale prices.

Site Development

We design and constructs mining facilities uniquely equipped with competitive power pricing and positive environmental positioning.

Carbon Advisory

MBC focusses on producing carbon-optimized Bitcoin. We are active participants in carbon markets globally. We help clients build operations that maximize returns while minimizing environmental impact.

Financing

MBC has supported several industry veterans in strengthening Bitcoin's infrastructure. We are constantly looking to support innovators with flexible financing solutions.
